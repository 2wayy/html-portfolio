Nisis Show Recommendation

My top 5 shows of all-time.

One Piece

Speaks for itself. Great adventure with a lot of different islands and critcs towards society. and the proof that the boarder between good and evil is sometimes smaller than it might seem on first glimpse.

Agents of Shield

What I like most about the show is, that you can relate to each characther, which usually made a great development in the course of the show as well.

Moon Knight

The mixture of the great acting and the serios background makes a great show, that is refreshingly different from other Marvel projects.

My Hero Academia

From 0 to Hero! Only if you believe in yourself, you will be able to accomplish something. Hard work will pay off, as proven by Izuku Midorya in My Hero Academia.

Friends

All time classic! A show that never gets boring and where you can still catch yourself laughing even at the 3rd watch-through.
